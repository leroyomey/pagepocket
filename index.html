Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 16, 2024

1. Introduction

At pagePocket, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information while using the pagePocket extension. By using the extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Saved Data: pagePocket allows you to save articles, links, and webpages. This data is stored locally in your browser’s storage and synced using Chrome’s cloud storage. We do not collect, access, or store this data on any external servers.

Anonymous Data: We do not collect personal or anonymous data. Your usage and interactions are private and remain within your browser storage.

3. How We Use Your Information

Locally Stored Data: The links, articles, and pages you save are stored locally within your browser and Chrome’s sync storage. This data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the extension and is never transmitted to third parties.

No Anonymous Data: pagePocket does not collect or use anonymous data for analytics or tracking purposes.

4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

No Third-Party Access: We do not sell, share, or transfer any personal information or saved content with third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.

Legal Requirements: We may only disclose data if required by law or if necessary to protect our rights or respond to legal processes.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. While the data is stored locally in your browser and synced using Chrome’s cloud storage, we recommend keeping your browser and Chrome account secure by using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication where available.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention: Your saved articles and links are stored locally in your browser as long as the extension is installed.

Data Deletion: You can delete individual articles or all saved data at any time. Uninstalling the pagePocket extension will also remove all stored data from your browser.

7. User Control

User Consent: By using pagePocket, you consent to the local storage of your saved content and understand that no external parties will have access to it.

Opting Out: If you no longer wish to use the extension or store data, you can uninstall the extension, which will remove all associated data from your browser.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the extension’s functionality or legal requirements. When we make changes, the “Effective Date” will be updated. We encourage users to review the Privacy Policy periodically.

Privacy Policy for ReadLater

Effective Date: 9/14/2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the ReadLater Chrome extension ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our extension.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) through the extension. However, the extension may use Chrome's built-in storage to save the following information locally on your device:

  • URLs and titles of saved web pages.

How We Use Your Information

All data saved by the extension is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. The data is used solely for the purpose of providing the "ReadLater" functionality, allowing you to save and access web pages at a later time.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services to collect, process, or store your data. Your saved articles are stored exclusively on your local device, and we do not share any data with third parties.

Your Choices

You can remove all saved articles by clearing your browser's local storage or uninstalling the extension. Once uninstalled, the extension will no longer store any data.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored on your local device from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" at the top will be updated accordingly.
